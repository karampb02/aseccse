How to Use

  1. Connect your Spotify account to access your playlists
  2. Connect your YouTube account to create playlists
  3. Load your Spotify playlists and select one to convert
  4. Wait while the app searches for each song on YouTube and creates your playlist
  5. Access your new YouTube playlist from the provided link

Note: YouTube has a daily API quota. Large playlists (50+ songs) may hit the limit. Some songs may not be found if they're not available on YouTube.
